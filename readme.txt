== Description ==

Product reviews and ratings by [Sparrow](https://getsparrow.io) helps you boost your store sales by enabling enhanced product reviews on your store using ***product reviews reminder emails***, ***review forms***, ***star ratings*** for the customers who have recently purchased from your store.

### Review Reminder Email

[Sparrow](https://getsparrow.io)'s review plugin for WooCommerce sends out review reminders to customers who have recently purchased from your store automatically. Be default the product review reminder emails are sent after ***14 days*** (can be configured) from the date of the order is marked 'completed' in WooCommerce dashboard.

* Increase sales with social proof
* Get great unique SEO content for your shop written by your customers
* Send automated email invitations asking customers who recently purchased for a review
* Personalize emails for each customer with built-in variables
* Works out of the box
* Sends a mobile-first responsive email
* Unsubscribe option (coming soon)
* Test emails before sending

### Product Review Form

[Sparrow](https://getsparrow.io) replaces the native reviews form with a better version. You can also select the position of the form, where you want to display the review form.

### Google Star Ratings

Sparrow automatically adds rich review snippets on your product pages. Star ratings are loved by search engines and helps you increasing organic CTR to your store. You can also choose to display star ratings on product category pages, under product title on single product page.

### Reviews Moderation

Sparrow allows advanced reviews moderation. You can even select whether to publish a review automatically or not. Filter out spam, restrict reviews based on word filters, etc.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/sparrow` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Go to Settings -> Sparrow, to configure the plugin
4. Add your access token. If you do not have one, you can generate by logging in to your account [here](http://app.getsparrow.io/login) and going [here](http://app.getsparrow.io/account/tokens)
5. That's all!

= How much does Sparrow cost? =

Sparrow is free to use for stores with up to 50 orders. You can check our detailed pricing on our website [here](https://getsparrow.io/pricing).

= Can I import my existing reviews? =

Yes! Please get in touch with our customer support team over in-app chat or drop us an email on support.

= Can I delete a review? =

Though, you can choose to hide a review; Deletion of reviews is not possible since it will affect the performance metrics. 

= What Woocommerce version do you support? =

Sparrow supports WooCommerce version 3.2.x and up.
