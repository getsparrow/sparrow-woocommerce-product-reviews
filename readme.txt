== Description ==

[Sparrow](https://getsoarrpw.io) helps you  boosting your store sales by enabling product reviews on your store using ***product reviews reminder emails***, ***review form***, ***star ratings*** for the customers who have recently purchased from your store.

### Product Review Form

[Sparrow](https://getsoarrpw.io) replaces the native reviews form with a better version. You can also select the position of the form, where you want to display the review form.

### Review Reminder Email

[Sparrow](https://getsparrow.io) sends out product review reminder emails after ***14 days*** (can be configured) of the purchase. Review forms are conversion optimised making it super-easy for your customers to leave product reviews.

### Star Rating

Display star ratings on product category pages, under product title on single product page.

### Reviews Moderation

Sparrow allows advanced reviews moderation. You can even select whether to publish a review automatically or not.




== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Use the Settings -> Sparrow screen to configure the plugin
4. Add your access token. If you do not have one, you can generate by loggin in to your account [here](http://app.getsparrow.io/login) and going [here](http://app.getsparrow.io/account/tokens)
5. That's all!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How much does Sparrow costs? =

Sparrow is free to use for stores with up to 50 orders. You can check our detailed pricing on our website.

= Can I import my existing reviews? =

This feature is currently not supported. We will introduce this feature soon.

= Can I delete a review? =

Though, you can choose to hide a review; Deletion of reviews is not possible since it will affect the performance metrics. 

= What Woocommerce version do you support? =

Sparrow supports WooCommerce version 3.2.x and up.
